Here is how to apply

Want a leave? Just follow along these simple steps to apply for an officially
acceptable leave application.


Approvals

All your leave plan should have Verbal Approval from


Client side Manager


Riflexions Manager

Application to be submitted prior to day of leave for approval.
Leave for period greater than 5 working days to be applied 1 Quarter in advance and approved
For any Sick or Emergency Leave it is mandatory to inform both the Managers on call and complete application process.


Application

Fill up the Leave application form & select "send me a copy of my responses".

Response

Forward the copy of response to all the stakeholders including your Managers
& team members.

Disclaimer: Any leaves taken without information to respective managers & HRD would lead to disciplinary action.
